About Us

Cafro Foods serves as the wholesale arm of the HM Foods Grocery Limited catering first to HM foods outlets and as well to small retail shops and corner shops across the UK and Europe. Cafro supplies groceries, frozen, fresh and dry foods - in bulk - to other small food retail businesses rather than directly to consumers and operates pickup/delivery services rather than on site cash and carry services. It also facilitates a centralized supply/restocking system for all three physical stores (and online store) of HM Foods Grocery Limited.
